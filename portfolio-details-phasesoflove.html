Description of Book

A book is never a book if not written with love and read with emotions. Tanya Arora pours down all her emotions and squeezes them with a few words to make them alive forever. She believes that life is very mortal to be inscribed with forever words but she also knows that one day when she will be breathing her last breath these words would remain alive as her remembrance.

Tanya Arora
